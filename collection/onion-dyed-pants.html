Onion Dyed Pants

Naturally dyed pants. White garments are hard to maintain and keep white, when they become too stained you can always give them a new colour. By pretreating the garment it can remove most of the stains, but otherwise naturally dyeing is a great solution. There are many ways to dye these days, but of course, we would recommend our natural dyeing techniques. These pants were recoloured with onion peels, giving off a deep rich yellow colour. Check out the academy for more dyeing methods and ingredients.

TECHNIQUE
Remake
WHAT YOU NEED
Red Onion peels from around 10-15 onions, Heat source, Big Pot, Prepared garment (Mordanted with Alum + Scoured), Hot Water, Sieve, Wooden spoon
DIFFICULTY
Beginner/Intermediate
