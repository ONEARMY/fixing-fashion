Avocado Rust Pants

Natural dyeing is basically playing with chemistry because many dyes react differently with simple ingredients. Case in point these pants. Originally dyed with avocado skins and stones a paintbrush was later used to paint rust water onto them and these grey stripes appeared.

TECHNIQUE
Decorate
WHAT YOU NEED
Avocado Skins +/or Peels, Heat source, Big Pot, Prepared garment (Mordanted with Alum + Scoured), Hot Water, Sieve, Wooden spoon, Rust mordant, Brush
DIFFICULTY
Intermediate
