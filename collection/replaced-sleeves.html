Replaced sleeves

Sleeves are always in the way and they are bound to get wrecked, ripped or stained. Why not replace them? If you have a shirt that has an unremovable stain or a bad design but the sleeves are still good you can use them and combine them with a good body portion, like this shirt here.

TECHNIQUE
Remake
WHAT YOU NEED
Two Shirts, Fabric crayon or Chalk, Measurement Tape, Ruler, Scissors, Pins, Sewingmachine, Sewing thread, Iron
